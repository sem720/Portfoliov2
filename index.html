About the Apprenticeship at Webasto

I decided to pursue an apprenticeship as an Industrial Business Manager at Webasto.
Webasto, headquartered in Stockdorf, Germany, is a global automotive supplier specializing in the development and production of sunroofs, panoramic roofs, convertible roofs, heating systems, battery systems, and charging solutions for all kind of vehicles.

During my time there, I gained experience with various applications, such as Microsoft CRM, PowerApps, Power Automate, VBA, UIPath, and many others. I also successfully contributed to developing a workflow application in Microsoft PowerApps (low-code), designed to replace an existing outsourced application.

About the Developer Akademie Course

During my Frontend Developer training at Developer Akademie, I gained a solid foundation in web development. I learned the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, applying these skills in projects such as creating responsive and interactive websites.

Key topics included optimizing websites for mobile devices, working with APIs and Firebase, and using Angular. The hands-on projects and close collaboration with experienced coaches have prepared me well for a career as a Frontend Developer.â€‹
